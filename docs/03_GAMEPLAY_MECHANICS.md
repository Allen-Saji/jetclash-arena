# Gameplay Mechanics

## Mode: Ranked Duel (MVP)
- 1v1
- Match duration: 2 minutes
- Win condition: highest score at timer end or first to kill cap

## Core systems
### Movement
- left/right movement
- jetpack thrust with fuel meter
- brief hover control

### Combat
- primary weapon (mid-range)
- secondary weapon (high impact, cooldown)
- one utility ability (dash or shield)

### Health and respawn
- HP pool
- death on zero HP
- fixed respawn delay (e.g., 2-3 sec)

### Pickups
- Health pack
- Ammo pack
- Speed boost
- Temporary damage buff

### Scoring
- kill: +100
- assist (if added later): +40
- objective bonus: optional
- death penalty: optional small score reduction

## Match UX
1. Queue accepted
2. 3-second countdown
3. Combat live
4. End summary (winner, score, credits earned)
5. Rematch / Back to lobby
